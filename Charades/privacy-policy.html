Privacy Policy

The app "Charades" collects and uses the data described in this document for analytics purpose only.

Unity Analytics

Our game uses Unity Analytics, a service provided by Unity Technologies, to help us understand how players interact with the game. This allows us to improve gameplay, fix issues, and make better design decisions.

What Data Is Collected

Unity Analytics may collect the following types of data:

  • Device information (such as model, operating system, and device ID)
  • Game usage data (such as session length, in-game events, and progression)
  • General location (country-level, based on IP address)

No personally identifiable information (PII), such as your name or email address, is collected by us through Unity Analytics.

Why We Collect This Data

We use this data to:

Analyze player behavior and improve the game experience

Identify and fix bugs and crashes

Measure overall game performance
