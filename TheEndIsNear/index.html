The End Is Near

Demons are raging, because their time will end soon

Goal:

You play as a human who tries to evade demons and sins to earn the possibility to have the eternal life, when this system ends.

Controls:

Touch on a screen so a joystic appears.

How to play:

  1. Dodge demons
  2. Pick up white circles, which explodes around and expel currently spawned demons

End game:

When you lose you see your age when you was filled with sin. However, as in real life, you can repent and start your fight against sins again.

Game Jam:

This hyper casual survival game created during Winter Jam by SoloDevelopment. The theme is "End is near".

How this idea was born:

Some time ago I read in Bible that during the end days demons will be raging the most, because their time will end soon.
